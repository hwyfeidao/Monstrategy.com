Monster Survivors

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed roguelike survival game where you must fight against endless waves of monsters. Each run is unique, with random enemy spawns, power-ups, and challenges to overcome.

Inspired by games like Vampire Survivors, this game combines fast-paced action with strategic character building. How long can you survive as the difficulty continuously increases?

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • Space: Pause the game
  • E or Enter: Select/Confirm
  • Esc: Back/Menu

Mobile Controls

  • Virtual Joystick: Appears on the left side of the screen for movement
  • Tap Buttons: Menu and action buttons on the right side
  • Swipe: Navigate menus and selection screens

Ragdoll Archers

About Ragdoll Archers

Ragdoll Archers is an archery game featuring bow and arrow-equipped stickmen with ragdoll physics. Fire arrows at a range of opponents while earning points to upgrade your abilities and ammunition. Play two-player PvP against your friend or team up with them to defeat a range of foes!

Released in August 2023 and developed by Ericetto, this game has earned an impressive 9.2 rating from over 1.6 million votes.

Casual 2 Player Arena Physics Co-op Archery Ragdoll Arcade

How to Play

Line up your shots carefully

Starting with basic arrows and baseline stats, you'll face various enemies. Some enemies are trickier than others due to armor, position, and other factors. Occasionally, you'll face giant enemies that act as mini-bosses, earning you lots of skulls when defeated.

Survive as long as possible

Your goal is to survive and earn skulls. The key to survival is hitting the flying apples:

  • Red apple = health
  • Green apple = stamina
  • Golden apple = Health and stamina

Customize your ammunition

There's an ever-increasing range of arrows to unlock as you play. Experiment with different types like electric, poison, balloon, and axe arrows to find which ones suit your style. You can increase your arrow slots to hold more at once.

Upgrade your abilities

Earn skulls by killing enemies, with rewards based on the power of defeated foes. Use these skulls to upgrade your health, stamina, damage, and more.

Game Controls

1 Player Mode

  • Mouse: Control the archer
  • Left Click: Draw and release to shoot
  • Spacebar: Jump

PvP Mode - Player 1

  • WASD: Control the archer
  • Left-Shift: Jump

PvP Mode - Player 2

  • Arrow Keys: Control the archer
  • Right-Shift: Jump

Pro Tips

  • Best Early Bow: The 3-shot bow is one of the best early game choices
  • Survival Strategy: Always aim for the apples to greatly increase your longevity
  • Platforms: Available on web browsers (desktop and mobile) and Android
